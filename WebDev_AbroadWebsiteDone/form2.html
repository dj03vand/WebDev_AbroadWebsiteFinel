What does it mean to be featured?

Share your adventure and influence another student with guidence and first hand experience you wish you had.


Don't you need more information to showcase my experience like the others I've seen on the website?

Yes we do! The point is everyone has a different approach to showcase themselves (pics, blog, vlog, combinations), we will collect more information after we contact.


In our rapid age of technology growth it has become easy to influence and inform people. Studying abroad is becoming extremley popular, the problem is who do we really share with? Our followers on social media, family, regular friends? Its our job as the "study abroad community" to change more lives within reach. What if someone missed out on an abroad experience, or was misinformed by the internet on realities of the country? We need to present what happened to us, first hand student accounts, the amazings, goods, bads, and uglies.
